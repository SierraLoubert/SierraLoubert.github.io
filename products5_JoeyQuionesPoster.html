Joey Quiones Poster

This poster Joey Quiones Poster was created for my Typography class, taught by Professor David Stairs. The project explored the use of scale, proportion, and hierarchy through the arrangement of text on a poster. We were tasked with creating a grid system to help align our designs. One of the main challenges in this project was fitting overlapping words into a single area while maintaining readability. The text was arranged in a clear hierarchy, and light colors were used to draw attention against the black background. The 45-degree tilt was inspired by a poster featured in our class textbook Typographic Design: Form and Communication edition 7, which also influenced the decision to tilt the grid and position the text accordingly. All layouts and design work were done in InDesign.
